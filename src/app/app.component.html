About Me

My name is Liam Grossmann. I'm a programmer and consultant who offers application and web development services. I've spent 25 years designing and implementing business solutions - primarily for the financial industry. My favourite design principle? pragmatism over idealism.

I was amazed and excited by the advancements made in web technology over the past decade. The release of new technologies such as HTML5, Web API, Type Script, and AngularJS coupled with the release of cloud based platforms such as Amazon Web Services and Microsoft Azure forced me to take stock. As a result of these advancements, I incorporated Cloud Coder Ltd in 2011.

Desktop applications have served businesses well for the past 20 years. It is now time to build the mobile business internet.
